How To Get To The Events

The Ceremony

(Victory bible church. International)

By Car

Venue is located on the Mallam - Lapaz stretch of the N1. At the Awoshie Baah yard Bus stop, venue can be seen.

By Public Transport

Pick any vehicle headed towards Awoshie or Mallam using the N1. Alight at the Awoshie Baah-Yard bus Stop.Venue would be seen as a huge building.

The Reception

(FIOFIO Events Centre)

By Car

Use the Mallam to Gbawe road towards Gbawe Bulemin. FIOFIO Event center is located on the bulemin stretch of the road.

By Public Transport

Pick any Vehicle to mallam station. Then Pick a loading taxi/trotro going towards Gbawe Bulemin.The events center is located on the bulemin rd.
